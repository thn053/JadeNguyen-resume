My background includes working in various roles where I developed tools to streamline financial processes, manage payroll, and forecast inventory needs. I am passionate about applying my skills in accounting and finance to real-world challenges.

Accounting & Finance Projects

Salon Revenue and Expense Tracker

While working at Polish Nails Spa, I independently developed a structured Excel workbook to manage daily revenue, service income, tips, and supply expenses. I categorized financial entries, used SUMIF and VLOOKUP functions to automate weekly summaries, and applied conditional formatting to flag irregular entries. The tool reduced manual calculation errors and allowed the business owner to make informed budgeting decisions with weekly exported PDF reports.

Tools: Microsoft Excel, manual receipt logging, PDF export

Skills: Bookkeeping, financial reporting, attention to detail

Employee Time Log & Payroll Estimator

At Daboba, I created an Excel-based payroll log that tracked employee clock-in and clock-out times. Using formulas like TEXT(), IF(), and MOD(), the tool calculated total hours worked per week and projected wages based on hourly rates. It also included conditional logic for overtime hours and flagged inconsistencies in punch times. This project minimized payroll disputes and helped managers estimate payroll costs quickly during scheduling.

Tools: Excel time functions, wage calculation formulas

Skills: Payroll basics, time management, logic-based spreadsheets

Inventory Cost Forecasting Tool

While working as a cashier at TOFU HOUSE, I designed a forecasting model to predict inventory needs and costs. I collected daily sales data, tracked restock frequency, and used historical patterns to estimate future demand. I implemented charts and trendlines to visualize usage over time, helping reduce over-ordering and minimizing spoilage. This tool was adopted by my manager to adjust purchasing decisions based on customer volume trends.

Tools: Excel charts, historical sales tracking, cost estimation formulas

Skills: Inventory planning, data analysis, demand forecasting
